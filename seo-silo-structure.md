# SEO SILO Structure for HNP.moscow

## Executive Summary
This document outlines a comprehensive SILO SEO strategy for Хромченков и Партнеры, a Moscow-based land law firm. The structure focuses on establishing topical authority in Russian land law while targeting specific geographic areas and service categories.

## SILO Architecture Overview

### Primary Pillar Themes (Level 1)
Based on current services, we identify 6 main pillar themes that will form the foundation of our SEO strategy:

1. **Право собственности на землю** (Land Ownership Rights)
2. **Земельные споры** (Land Disputes)
3. **Сделки с земельными участками** (Land Transactions)
4. **Наследство земельных участков** (Land Inheritance)
5. **Дачная амнистия** (Dacha Amnesty)
6. **Кадастровая стоимость** (Cadastral Value)

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Pillar to Cluster Linking
- Each pillar page links to all its cluster pages (minimum 6-8 links)
- Clusters link back to their pillar (canonical link)
- Related clusters link to each other where relevant

### Geographic Cross-Linking
- Geographic pages link to relevant service pillars
- Cross-reference similar services in different districts

### Authority Linking
- Resource pages link to relevant pillar pages
- Case studies link to related service pages
- Blog posts link to appropriate pillar/cluster pages

## Technical SEO Implementation

### URL Structure
- Use hyphens (not underscores) in URLs
- Keep URLs short (under 100 characters)
- Include target keywords where natural
- Use Cyrillic transliteration for Russian content

### Breadcrumbs Implementation
```html
<nav aria-label="breadcrumb">
  <ol itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/BreadcrumbList">
    <li itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">
      <a itemprop="item" href="/"><span itemprop="name">Главная</span></a>
      <meta itemprop="position" content="1">
    </li>
    <li itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">
      <a itemprop="item" href="/pravo-sobstvennosti/"><span itemprop="name">Право собственности</span></a>
      <meta itemprop="position" content="2">
    </li>
    <li itemprop="itemListElement" itemscope itemtype="http://schema.org/ListItem">
      <span itemprop="name">Приватизация</span>
      <meta itemprop="position" content="3">
    </li>
  </ol>
</nav>
```

### Schema Markup Implementation
- **Organization Schema** for company information
- **LocalBusiness Schema** for geographic targeting
- **Service Schema** for each service offering
- **FAQPage Schema** for frequently asked questions
- **Article Schema** for blog posts and resources
